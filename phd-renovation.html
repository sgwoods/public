Current state

Primary goal

Re-establish one integrated constraint-solver line that reproduces the PhD results, covers other historical result families, and can stand in as the gold-standard codebase.

What is already solid

All four maintained historical solver snapshots load on SBCL. The thesis ij2 / ij3 / ij4 validation path is executable and CI-protected. AO baseline assertions now cover qcsp3 and May29 cases 1-3 plus the legacy test4 wrappers, alex keeps its revise baseline, ff provenance is checked, and both the older ADT archive and the earliest DREV-era T-series archive are integrity-checkable.

What M1 means

qcsp3 has now crossed the first real integrated-line gate: it holds the PhD baseline, the asserted AO baseline, and the documented ff relationship under one supported executable story. The remaining archive-heavy families are explicitly post-M1, with terrain the only major blocked lane outside it.

What comes next

The current mainline is still 0.3.x operator documentation and handoff polish, but the ADT batch bridge has now started in earnest: a first csp-based ij2 probe runs and currently diverges from the preserved non-timing metrics. Tracing that divergence is the next concrete integration task.
