Current state

Primary goal

Re-establish one integrated constraint-solver line that reproduces the PhD results, covers other historical result families, and can stand in as the gold-standard codebase.

What is already solid

All four maintained historical solver snapshots load on SBCL. The thesis ij2 / ij3 / ij4 validation path is executable and CI-protected. AO baseline assertions and ff provenance checks are part of the supported validation spine.

What M1 means

qcsp3 becomes the first real integrated line when it holds the PhD baseline, the asserted AO baseline, and the documented ff relationship under one supported executable story.
