# Incoming

Place raw unsorted finds here first.

This folder can also hold campaign-style research outputs when they are part of the archive method, not just temporary scratch notes.

Typical items:

- PDFs
- screenshots
- HTML captures
- text notes
- scanned documents
- recovered media files
- named lead clusters
- themed research passes
- follow-up capture campaigns such as site gaps, investor trails, or exit evidence

The goal of `incoming/` is capture-before-classification: keep the material safe first, then process it into `historic/`.
