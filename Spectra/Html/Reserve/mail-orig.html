Error in parameters to http://www.fwi.uva.nl/cgi-bin/mail_form

Report: mail recipient address was left empty
Usage: http://www.fwi.uva.nl/cgi-bin/mail_form?parameters

The parameters are separated by ampersand (&) characters and should not contain spaces or equal (=) characters other than for assignments (see below).

Possible parameters are:
sendto=email_address
sendsubj=subject
gotourl=url

sendto and gotourl are essential. Leaving either empty will result in mail not being sent. If the sendsubj is left empty, one is filled in. Other fields will be left unmodified and will be listed in the mailed message body.

Example:
http://www.fwi.uva.nl/cgi-bin/mail_form?sendto=sgwoods@logos.uwaterloo.ca&gotourl=http://www.fwi.uva.nl/
