Background

I am currently a Ph.D Candidate in Computer Science at the University of Waterloo. I am a member of the Logic Programming and Artificial Intelligence Group (LPAIG), and am primarily interested in reverse engineering, constraint satisfaction, plan recognition and planning. My supervisor is Dr. Qiang Yang (*). I completed my M.Math degree at Waterloo in 1991 with the submission of my Master's thesis entitled "An implementation and evaluation of a hierarchical non-linear planner"(Abtweak). Years ago I did my undergraduate work at the University of Saskatchewan, completing my B.Sc. (Computer Science) degree in 1987. I am currently affiliated with the Information Technology Research Centre (ITRC).
