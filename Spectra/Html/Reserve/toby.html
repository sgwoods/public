Background

For years after my birth, I was a young boy in Vancouver, B.C. Forced by the government to attend public school for over a decade, I eventually went to university to study mathematics, computer science, and English literature. I graduated with a B.Sc. in pure mathematics from Simon Fraser University, and am currently a graduate student in computer science here at the University of Waterloo.
