ABTWEAK Home Page

AbTweak is a complete hierarchical, non-linear planner that extends David Chapman's (MIT 1986) "Tweak" planner as described by Yang (Waterloo) and Tenenberg (Rochester) in 1989. This implementation by Steven Woods (1991 Masters Thesis) includes a complete search strategy suited to abstraction hierarchies known as LEFT-WEDGE (Woods 1991). This planner and related work predates that of SNLP.
