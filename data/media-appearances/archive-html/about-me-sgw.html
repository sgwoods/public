I am a team builder, engineering leader, founder and X-Googler. Currently I am Partner and CTO at Inovia Capital. A long time ago I co-founded a few companies including Quack.com which we sold to a very large internet company of ill-repute.

I hail from, and spend a lot of time up in northern Saskatchewan. I enjoy the wilderness of the north, and take the odd photograph while up there.

I currently reside in the countryside with my family near Waterloo, Ontario, Canada.

I have a very active interest in recent developments in artificial intelligence and in a different life finished a PhD on the topic at the University of Waterloo.
