# Kinitos / NeoEdge Networks Archive

This directory is the intake area for the public archive project covering the Kinitos company period and its later NeoEdge Networks refocus.

Use the lane folders below so recovered material keeps its original context:

- `memories/`: recollections, timeline notes, and narrative context
- `demos/`: screenshots, recordings, demo packages, and walkthrough material
- `artifacts/`: documents, diagrams, archives, binaries, and other non-code items
- `code/`: source trees, build notes, and reconstruction material

When dates, authorship, or provenance are uncertain, keep that uncertainty explicit in the accompanying notes instead of normalizing it away.

This intake area can also hold lightweight campaign outputs when they help the archive stay understandable, such as:

- named source-lead clusters
- themed research passes
- archived site-capture follow-up lists
- investor, partner, or exit-trail campaign notes

Current canonical coordination outputs for the active archive live in `public/kinitos-neoedge/`:

- `project-manifest.json`
- `source-manifest.json`
- `public-handoff.json`
- project page: `public/kinitos-neoedge.html`
